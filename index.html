About NeonVerse Radio

NeonVerse Radio is an independent online radio station based in Toronto, Canada, streaming synthwave, electronic and futuristic music 24 hours a day. Inspired by cyberpunk aesthetics and modern electronic soundscapes, NeonVerse Radio explores the sound of the future city through modern electronic music.

The station features emerging artists, experimental productions and next-generation electronic music styles inspired by neon-lit cityscapes and digital worlds.
