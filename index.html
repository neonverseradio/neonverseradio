About NeonVerse Radio

NeonVerse Radio is an independent online radio station based in Toronto, Canada, streaming synthwave, electronic and futuristic music 24 hours a day. Inspired by cyberpunk aesthetics and modern electronic soundscapes, NeonVerse Radio delivers the sound of the future city through continuous synthwave and electronic music streaming.

The station features emerging artists, experimental productions and next-generation electronic music styles inspired by neon-lit cityscapes and digital worlds.
