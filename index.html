About NeonVerse Radio

NeonVerse Radio - The Sound of Future City - is an independent 24/7 online radio station based in Toronto, Canada, Streaming synthwave, synthpop, electronic, and cyberpunk music 24 hours a day, NeonVerse Radio delivers nonstop futuristic soundscapes, night drive energy, and immersive electronic vibes.

Listen live worldwide and experience a continuous mix of emerging indie artists, experimental productions, and modern electronic music inspired by neon-lit cityscapes and digital worlds.
